Quick recipe for Noodles!

  1. Ingredients: 12 oz noodles (any kind), 1 tbsp oil, 1 lb ground beef/pork/chicken, 1 onion (sliced), 2 cloves garlic (sliced), 3 cups spinach, 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp dark soy sauce, 2 tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp honey, 2 tsp chili oil (optional).

World's Longest Noodle: A Chinese company created a single noodle that was 3,084 meters long. Not a Marco Polo Invention: Contrary to popular belief, Marco Polo did not bring noodles to Italy; pasta existed in Europe prior to his travels. Top 3 Consumers: The World Instant Noodles Association reports the top consumers are Vietnam, South Korea, and Thailand. "Noodle" vs. Pasta: While both are unleavened doughs, noodles are historically associated with Asia, while pasta is associated with Italy.
